🪁 Kite — 3D

The kite flies the whole wind window: park it at the edge and it barely pulls, dive it through the power zone (the hot arcs, low and downwind) and it hauls. Twist the phone (yaw) to point the board upwind / crosswind / downwind — pinch too high and you stall. To jump like the pros: ride powered with the kite at 10, send it hard past 12, pull the bar — then steer it back forward to land with speed.
