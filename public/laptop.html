Kite bar — laptop display

📱 Connect your phone

  1. Run the server on your laptop: npm start
  2. Make sure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi network
  3. Scan the QR code printed in the terminal, or open https://<your-laptop-ip>:8443/phone on your phone
  4. Accept the self-signed certificate warning
  5. Tap Start streaming on the phone page
  6. Hold the phone in landscape grip — the kite above should move in real time

The status indicator at the top of the panel turns green when the stream is live.

🎮 Play in game mode

  1. Connect your phone first (see left)
  2. Optionally tweak the steering axis, scale, and wind settings below
  3. Click the button below to open the full game

Game mode adds a score, wind spots, and jump mechanics. Your mapping settings carry over automatically.
